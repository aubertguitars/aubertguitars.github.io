About

Since January 2015, I've been learning web development with the Founders & Coders organisation in East London. I believe that to be effective in digital businesses, it really helps to have a technical understanding. Even better if you know enough to create things yourself. I was frustrated with my lack of coding knowledge, so I set out to do something about it. Do check out the course if any of that resonated with you.


Before that I took part in the New Entrepreneurs Foundation cohort of 2014 .
The Foundation is creating a new generation of entrepreneurs who will play a role in Britain’s future growth and create new market-leading businesses.
A highly selective group of the UK’s 30 brightest and most entrepreneurially minded young people were chosen to get hands-on experience, skills and networks to build scalable businesses.


Previously, I worked in Investment Banking, started an events business, lead a sales team for a startup and got an Economics Bsc from the University of Bath.
